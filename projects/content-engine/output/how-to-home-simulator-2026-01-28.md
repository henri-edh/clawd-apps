## Step 2: Design Your Space

A 10ft x 12ft space is ideal, but 10ft x 10ft works too. Clear out the area, remove obstacles, and ensure you have at least 9ft of ceiling height for your swing.

### Space Requirements
- Minimum width: 10ft
- Minimum depth: 12ft
- Minimum height: 9ft
- Ideal: 12ft x 14ft x 10ft

### Lighting
- Diffuse lighting preferred
- Avoid direct glare on screen/launch monitor
- Warm white LED strips work well

### Flooring
- Hitting mat: Essential for protecting your floors
- Turf or golf carpet for realistic feel
- Putting green area if space allows

## Step 4: Setup Your Screen/Projector

An impact screen costs around $250 and handles the ball well. Pair it with a 1080p projector for $500. Mount the projector 8-10ft from the screen for the best image size.

### Screen Options
- **Hitting Screen**: Durable, reflects projector well
- **Impact Screen**: Quieter, expensive
- **White Wall**: Cheapest, need to be smooth

### Projector Options
- **4K Projector**: Sharp image, expensive
- **1080p Projector**: Good balance of price/quality
- **Throw Distance**: Match to your room depth

### Setup Tips
- Projector distance: 8-12ft from screen
- Mount at correct height for your hitting zone
- Keystone correction for straight image

## Step 5: Connect Your Launch Monitor

Place your Mevo+ 4-6ft directly behind your hitting zone. Ensure it's level using the built-in bubble level. Connect via Bluetooth to E6 CONNECT or your preferred simulator software.

### FlightScope Mevo+ Setup
1. Position Mevo+ 4-6ft behind ball on hitting mat
2. Level the device (includes level in box)
3. Connect to E6 CONNECT or Golf Club 2019
4. Test with a few shots to verify data
5. Adjust position if needed for accurate reads

### Calibration
- Follow manufacturer instructions exactly
- Check firmware is updated
- Verify no interference from other electronics

## Step 6: Fine-Tune Your Experience

Start with E6 CONNECT's tutorial to learn the interface. Practice on easier courses first. Track your improvement using Mevo+'s data over time.

### Audio
- External speakers for immersion
- Golf-specific sound effects optional

### Seating
- Comfortable chair at correct distance
- Can swivel to watch ball flight on screen

### Organization
- Club storage nearby
- Ball retriever or multiple balls ready
- Scorecard or pen for tracking

## Pro Tips

- **Start simple**: You can always upgrade screen/projector later
- **Use existing iPad/tablet**: Don't count on buying one in budget
- **Shop sales**: Projectors often go on sale
- **DIY screen**: A simple white sheet or paint can work temporarily
- **E6 CONNECT trial**: Try before buying annual subscription

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- **Buying too expensive launch monitor**: You don't need $10k+ TrackMan
- **Underestimating space**: Measure twice, buy once
- **Poor lighting**: Glare ruins the experience
- **Ignoring setup**: Calibration matters for accuracy
- **Forgetting software budget**: E6 CONNECT is $500/year
